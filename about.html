About Our Organization

Empowering communities through ethical values, education, and women's upliftment

Mustafa Kamaal Education and Welfare Society (Indore, M.P., India) is a non-profit organization dedicated to simplifying marriages, promoting education, and empowering women—all while upholding Islamic values and social welfare principles.

Our Mission

Dowry-Free Marriages

Eliminate dowry and make marriages simple, affordable, and Sunnah-compliant

Education Support

Support students in pursuing higher education through scholarships and guidance

Women Empowerment

Empower women (Mahila Sashaktikaran) by fighting against dowry

Employment

Generate lawful employment opportunities to uplift communities

What We Do

1. Ethical & Affordable Marriages

  • Dowry-Free Nikah: Promoting marriages without financial burdens
  • Matchmaking (Ahkamaat-e-Kafu): Ensuring compatibility based on Islamic principles
  • Marriage Counseling: Pre and post-marriage guidance for couples

2. Education & Career Guidance

  • Scholarships & Financial Aid for deserving students
  • Career Counseling for youth to choose the right profession
  • Parent & Student Counseling to address academic challenges

3. Women's Empowerment (Mahila Sashaktikaran)

  • Anti-Dowry Awareness: Campaigns against dowry exploitation
  • Skill Development & Employment: Helping women achieve financial stability
  • Legal Support: Assisting women in marital and family disputes

4. Employment & Social Welfare

  • Creating sustainable income sources for unemployed individuals
  • Encouraging halal (lawful) earnings through skill-based opportunities

Our Vision

Marriages are simple, dignified, and free from greed
No student is deprived of education due to poverty
Women are respected, independent, and protected from exploitation
Every individual has access to lawful employment
